How to use:

You can enter any query to match on rocket type, rocket ID, rocket name, launch site type, launch site id, launch site name, and launch details. You can also enter multiple formats of dates to match on launch dates, and numbers to match on launch number as well as any values contained in details. Searching for things like "success", "succeed", "failure", "fail", "succeeded", and "failed" will not only search for these terms within the details, but also check launches' success statuses. Click the result's launch number header to view a detail page with more information including links to videos, news articles, and images.
